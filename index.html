About Us

We are an indie game studio focused on creating atmospheric and immersive horror experiences. With limited resources but a strong creative vision, we aim to craft games that rely on tension, storytelling, and thoughtful design rather than large-scale production.

Every project is built with dedication, experimentation, and a passion for the horror genre.

Our mission is to deliver simple yet memorable horror experiences and continue improving with each release.
